FAQs

Common Questions

How do I place an order?

The easiest way is WhatsApp — just tap the green button on this page or any product page. You can also call us or fill the form above.

Do you deliver outside Tamil Nadu?

Yes! We deliver PAN India via Speed Post and trusted courier services. Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days.

What are the minimum order quantities?

No minimums for retail orders — even a single 100g packet is welcome. For bulk/wholesale orders, contact us for pricing.

How do I pay for my order?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery for select pin codes. Payment details shared on WhatsApp after order confirmation.
