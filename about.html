How It Began

Born in a Grandmother's Kitchen

It started with the smell of roasting coriander on a cast-iron pan, the sound of a stone grinder humming in the early morning, and a grandmother who believed that the quality of a meal began with the quality of its spice.

In 1985, Rajammal began blending masalas in her home in Madurai, selling small packets to neighbours. Word spread faster than she could grind. By 2005, her son expanded to a small stone-mill facility. Today, her grandchildren carry that same obsession with purity into every batch we make.

What Sets Us Apart

Why We Are Different

In a world of mass-produced blends, we're a stubborn holdout for the old way of doing things.

🌾

Direct Farm Partnerships

We visit our farms personally. We know the farmers by name. This isn't marketing — it's how we ensure every chilli and every seed meets our standard before it reaches our mill.

Low-Heat Processing

We grind at low temperatures to protect volatile oils and nutrients that high-speed industrial machines destroy. The result: brighter colour, deeper aroma, more flavour.

Every batch is tested for adulteration, pesticide residue, and moisture before packing. We reject anything that doesn't meet our standards — no exceptions.

The Journey

Farm to Kitchen — Step by Step

Transparency is our flavour. Here's exactly how your masala is made.

🌱

Step 1 – Farm Sourcing

We source directly from certified farms in Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Kerala. Our team visits each farm at harvest to inspect quality, ripeness, and farming practices before purchasing.

☀️

Step 2 – Sun Drying

Freshly harvested spices are spread on clean raised platforms and sun-dried naturally for 3–7 days. This locks in natural oils and reduces moisture without using artificial dryers.

🔍

Step 3 – Quality Inspection

Every batch is hand-sorted to remove damaged, discoloured, or imperfect pieces. We then send samples to a third-party lab for purity and residue testing before grinding.

🪨

Step 4 – Stone Grinding

Spices are ground in traditional granite stone mills at low speed and low temperature. This preserves the essential oils, aroma compounds, and flavour that industrial grinders destroy.

📦

Step 5 – Airtight Packing

Freshly ground masala is packed immediately in multi-layer moisture-proof, airtight pouches. Each packet is heat-sealed and labelled with batch date and ingredients for full traceability.

🚚

Step 6 – Fast Delivery

Orders are dispatched within 24 hours of packing. We deliver PAN India through trusted courier partners, ensuring your masala arrives fresh — and your kitchen smells amazing.
